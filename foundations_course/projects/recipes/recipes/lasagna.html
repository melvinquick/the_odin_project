Lasagna



Description

This classic lasagna recipe combines layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese for a hearty and satisfying meal. Perfect for a weeknight dinner or special occasion, this dish is sure to become a family favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup lasagna noodles
  • 1 package frozen spinach, thawed and drained
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Italian seasonings (basil, oregano, thyme)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F.
  2. Cook lasagna noodles according to package directions.
  3. Brown ground beef with onion and garlic, then add crushed tomatoes and Italian seasonings. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  4. Combine ricotta cheese, mozzarella cheese, and Parmesan cheese in a bowl.
  5. Sauté spinach and mushrooms in a pan until tender.
  6. Assemble lasagna by layering meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, and sautéed spinach and mushrooms. Top with mozzarella cheese.
  7. Bake for 45 minutes, or until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Let it rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing and serving.
