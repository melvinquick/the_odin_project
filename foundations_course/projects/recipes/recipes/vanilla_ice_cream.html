Vanilla Ice Cream



Description

This rich and creamy vanilla ice cream is a classic dessert made with simple ingredients and a touch of sweetness. Perfect for hot summer days or as a sweet treat any time of the year.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. In a medium saucepan, combine heavy cream, whole milk, and granulated sugar. Heat over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until sugar has dissolved and mixture is hot but not boiling.
  2. Remove from heat and stir in vanilla extract.
  3. Let mixture cool to room temperature, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours or overnight.
  4. Pour mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to manufacturer's instructions.
  5. Freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
