Salad



Description

This refreshing salad combines crisp lettuce, juicy vegetables, and tangy dressing for a light and healthy snack or side dish. Perfect for a quick lunch or dinner accompaniment, this salad is customizable to your favorite ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mixed greens (lettuce, arugula, spinach)
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cup cucumber, sliced
  • 1/2 cup carrots, peeled and grated
  • 1/4 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh herbs (parsley, basil, dill)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, combine mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, and carrots.
  2. Top with crumbled feta cheese and chopped fresh herbs.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil and balsamic vinegar.
  4. Drizzle dressing over salad and toss to combine.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
